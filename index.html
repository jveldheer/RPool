12-WEEK ALL-AMERICAN COMBINE PREP
What Wins Your 40
First 10 yd: Shins and torso forward, push the ground back, big arm swing
Body Stiffness: Ankles like springs, hips locked in, ribcage stacked
Force & Velocity: Heavy trap bar and box squat early week, fast jumps and flying 10s later
Hamstring Armor: Nordics, isometrics, RDLs, smart sprint progressions
Freshness: Right work, well rested
12-Week Timeline
Week 1: Reset and teach - Learn starts, light volume
Weeks 2-4: Acceleration base - Build 10-20 yd speed and joint capacity
Weeks 5-7: Accel + MaxV build - Add wickets and flying 10s
Weeks 8-10: Speed + power peak - Low volume, high quality, plenty of rest
Weeks 11-12: Taper - Keep speed, drop volume, sharpen
Weekly Layout (4 Training Days)
Monday: Acceleration + Lower A Lift (60 min)
Tuesday: Upper A Lift + Sled Armor (60 min)
Thursday: MaxV Mechanics + Lower B Lift (60 min)
Saturday: Upper B Lift + Isometrics and Power (60 min)

Wed, Fri, Sun: Recovery emphasis with short mobility
⚠️ Red Flags - Automatic Changes
• Sleep <8.5 h: Reduce lifting intensity -15%, add 20-30 min nap
• New joint pain: Stop the move, swap to pain-free pattern
• Bodyweight swings >5 lb in 24h: Check fluids, carbs, salt
• Hamstring tightness: Skip flying 10s, bike 5×1 min easy

40-YARD DASH MECHANICS

Start Setup
• Feet hip width, front foot 2-3 foot lengths behind line
• Back toe even with mid foot of front
• Front hand down under shoulder, back hand set near hip
• Eyes down 2-3 yd ahead, neck neutral, chin tucked
First 10 Yards
• Push the ground back, do not reach
• Shins and torso at same angle, about 45°
• Big arm from back hip to front cheek
• 4-6 strong steps, each step a bit taller than the last
10-20 Yards
• Keep pushing, do not pop up too soon
• Maintain forward lean, head stays neutral
20-40 Yards
• Transition to tall, quick, and stiff
• Feet under hips, bounce off the ground
• Ribcage stacked over pelvis
Common Fixes
If you stumble: Shorten first step, cue "push, do not reach"
If you pop up too soon: "Eyes down for first 10 yd" and "drive knees, do not lift chest"
If you spin tires: Bigger arm swing and harder push on step 1-2
Video Checklist
• Film from side at hip height
• Check shin angle on step 1-3
• From behind, check foot strikes near midline
• Time 10 yd and flying 10 weekly to see trends
