Sprint Mechanics

3-Point Start Position:

  • Down hand 1 hand-length behind line
  • Hips higher than shoulders, weight 60/40 forward
  • Eyes down 2-3 yards ahead

First 10 Yards:

  • Push the ground back, do not reach
  • Shins and torso at same angle (~45°)
  • Big arm drive from back hip to front cheek

Race Morning Warmup (25-30 min)

  1. General Warmup (5 min): Easy jog or brisk walk
  2. Dynamic Mobility: A-march, A-skip, B-skip, Leg swings
  3. Ankle Activation: Pogo jumps, Wall ankle mobs
  4. Progressive Build-ups: 10 yd @70%, 20 yd @80%, 30 yd @85%
  5. Practice Starts: 2-3 practice starts at 10 yd
  6. Rehearsal Run: 1 × 20 yd at 90%
  7. Final Preparation: Breathe and spike up. You're ready!
